## 📝 How to Create Slides

This tool uses a plain text prompt to build your deck. Follow these steps to generate a presentation:

1. Open the application.
2. Select the "New Project" button.
3. Type a topic into the text box. Be specific. For example, write "Business strategy for a coffee shop" instead of just "Coffee shop."
4. Select a style preference from the provided list.
5. Click the "Generate" button.
6. Wait for the software to build the structure.

## 💡 Tips for Better Results

*   **Be Descriptive:** Include the goal of the presentation in your prompt. If you need a sales pitch, mention your target audience.
*   **Check the Layout:** Review each slide for clarity. You can drag and drop elements to adjust the look.
*   **Use High-Quality Assets:** You can upload your own company logo or custom images to replace the placeholders.
*   **Check Spelling:** Always review the generated text for typos before you share the deck.

## 📂 Managing Projects

The application stores your files in a folder named "Presentations" within your Documents directory. You can organize your files into sub-folders if you manage many projects. To open a past project, select "Open" from the main menu and locate the file on your computer.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

*   **Application wont open:** Restart your computer and try to run the installer again.
*   **Slides look empty:** Check your internet connection. The AI needs a connection to build the slide assets.
*   **Export fails:** Ensure you have enough disk space on your drive.
*   **Slow performance:** Close unnecessary programs like web browsers or video players while you use the software.
